Get familiar With Online Judges

In order To boost up your practice , you should start problem solving ASAP. But where can you find such problems? Also who will judge your work? The answwr is online judges . Basically there are numerous different platforms where you get number of problems to solve doing programming . You can submit your answer and it will be judgesd automatically . These platforms also hosts programming contests , and also keeps track of your performance . Let's get familiar with some of these platforms .

Some of the most popular online judges :

Codeforces

Codeforces is one of the most popular online judges because of it's various range of problemset and Regular short contests .

CodeChef

CodeChef is a popular indian online judge . It has various range of problems and Regular short as well as long contests .

TopCoder

TopCoder is one of the most popular online judges because of it's various range of problemset and Regular short contests .

AtCoder

AtCoder is one of the most popular online judges because of it's various range of problemset and Regular short contests .

Toph

Toph is a popular Bangladeshi Online Judge .

UVa

UVa is famous for it's numerous set of problems,Though they don't conduct any contests.

URI

URI is one of the coolest beginner friendly online judge that you should definitely check out!

HackerRank

Hackerrank is basically a platform to improve your programming skill. Here you will find everything you need while learning a new concept.
